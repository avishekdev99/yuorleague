Our Basketball Pools

Basketball Pick ‘Em Pools

Members choose a winner from each game each week

  • Pick a winner from every game
  • Rankings are calculated after each round and for the season
  • Choose your own settings: Number of games, playoff mode, automatic pick assignment for missed picks and much more

Basketball Knockout Pools

Members choose a different team each week until only one winner remains!

  • Each member chooses one team each week
  • If they win you progress to the next round.
  • If they lose you are eliminated
  • Pick your own settings: Mulligans, automatic assignment for missed picks etc.

Basketball Playoff Pools

Choose your playoff teams

  • Make your playoff picks ranked 1-16
  • Get points each time a team wins i.e your number 1 wins-16 points
  • Apply your multipliers tactically
  • The most points wins
